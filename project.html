RMP (Rotation, Mutation, and Promotion) 🔄

About Project

RMP (Rotation, Mutation, and Promotion) is a web-based employee career movement management system developed for PT Petrokimia Gresik to streamline the submission, approval, and monitoring of Rotation, Mutation, and Promotion (RMP) proposals in accordance with the company's grading policies. The platform enables eligible employees (Grade 3 and above) to submit RMP proposals digitally while allowing authorized stakeholders to review, evaluate, and approve requests through a structured workflow.


The system supports the complete RMP document lifecycle, including proposal submission, multi-level evaluation and approval, final RMP letter generation, and real-time proposal tracking. It also provides dedicated modules for proposal management, evaluation, final letter processing, and monitoring, improving administrative efficiency, transparency, and document traceability throughout the employee career movement process.

The system supports seven main user roles:

👨‍💼 Administrator
📝 Drafter
🔎 Reviewer
📤 Proposer
✅ Approver
💰 Remuneration
👤 Employee

RMP Workflow

The RMP workflow follows a structured review, approval, and evaluation process to ensure every Rotation, Mutation, and Promotion proposal is properly assessed, authorized, and documented before being officially implemented.

  • 📝 Proposal Submission – Eligible employees (Grade 3 and above) create and submit an RMP proposal. Proposals can also be saved as drafts before submission.
  • 🔎 Proposal Review – The Proposer (Grade 2) reviews the submitted proposal for completeness and accuracy before forwarding it to the next approval stage.
  • ✅ Approval – The Approver (Grade 1) reviews and authorizes eligible proposals. Approved proposals proceed as final proposals, while rejected proposals are returned for revision.
  • 📋 HR Evaluation – The SDM Administrator evaluates the approved proposal to ensure compliance with organizational policies and workforce requirements.
  • 📥 Proposal Acceptance – Approved proposals are accepted and recorded for further processing. Rejected proposals are returned to the applicant.
  • 📑 Temporary Decree Preparation – The SDM Administrator adds the approved proposal to the Temporary Decree (SK Sementara) for further evaluation.
  • 📄 Temporary Decree Issuance – The SDM Administrator generates the Temporary Decree (SK Sementara), which remains under evaluation until final approval is obtained.
  • ✔️ SK Approval – The Temporary Decree is reviewed and officially approved. If rejected, it may be removed from the evaluation process for revision.
  • 🚀 PETROSTAR Integration – Once the Temporary Decree is approved, the finalized RMP data is automatically synchronized with the PETROSTAR system.

RMP (Rotation, Mutation, and Promotion) 🔄

About Project

The RMP (Rotation, Mutation, and Promotion) System is a web-based employee career movement management platform implemented for PT Pupuk Kujang Cikampek. The system is based on the proven RMP framework developed by PT Petrokimia Gresik and has been adapted to align with PT Pupuk Kujang Cikampek's organizational structure, business processes, and operational requirements. It streamlines the submission, approval, and monitoring of Rotation, Mutation, and Promotion (RMP) proposals through a structured digital workflow. The platform enables authorized employees to submit RMP proposals electronically while allowing relevant stakeholders to review, evaluate, and approve requests efficiently, improving transparency, consistency, and overall workforce management.


The system supports the complete RMP document lifecycle, including proposal submission, multi-level evaluation and approval, final RMP letter generation, and real-time proposal tracking. It also provides dedicated modules for proposal management, evaluation, final letter processing, and monitoring, improving administrative efficiency, transparency, and document traceability throughout the employee career movement process.

The system supports seven main user roles:

👨‍💼 Administrator
📝 Drafter
🔎 Reviewer
📤 Proposer
✅ Approver
💰 Remuneration
👤 Employee

RMP Workflow

The RMP workflow follows a structured review, approval, and evaluation process to ensure every Rotation, Mutation, and Promotion proposal is properly assessed, authorized, and documented before being officially implemented.

  • 📝 Proposal Submission – Eligible employees (Grade 3 and above) create and submit an RMP proposal. Proposals can also be saved as drafts before submission.
  • 🔎 Proposal Review – The Proposer (Grade 2) reviews the submitted proposal for completeness and accuracy before forwarding it to the next approval stage.
  • ✅ Approval – The Approver (Grade 1) reviews and authorizes eligible proposals. Approved proposals proceed as final proposals, while rejected proposals are returned for revision.
  • 📋 HR Evaluation – The SDM Administrator evaluates the approved proposal to ensure compliance with organizational policies and workforce requirements.
  • 📥 Proposal Acceptance – Approved proposals are accepted and recorded for further processing. Rejected proposals are returned to the applicant.
  • 📑 Temporary Decree Preparation – The SDM Administrator adds the approved proposal to the Temporary Decree (SK Sementara) for further evaluation.
  • 📄 Temporary Decree Issuance – The SDM Administrator generates the Temporary Decree (SK Sementara), which remains under evaluation until final approval is obtained.
  • ✔️ SK Approval – The Temporary Decree is reviewed and officially approved. If rejected, it may be removed from the evaluation process for revision.
  • 🚀 PETROSTAR Integration – Once the Temporary Decree is approved, the finalized RMP data is automatically synchronized with the PETROSTAR system.

SUPER (Sistem Uraian Pekerjaan) 📑

About Project

SUPER (Sistem Uraian Pekerjaan) is a web-based platform implemented for PT Pupuk Kujang Cikampek by adopting and customizing the SUPER solution previously developed for PT Petrokimia Gresik. The system has been tailored to align with PT Pupuk Kujang Cikampek's organizational structure, business processes, and operational requirements while maintaining the core functionality of standardized job description management. It streamlines the submission, review, approval, and monitoring of employee job descriptions through a structured digital workflow. The platform enables employees to prepare and submit job description documents using standardized templates, while authorized reviewers and approvers can efficiently evaluate, approve, and manage submissions, improving consistency, transparency, and overall human resource administration.


The system includes role-based access control (RBAC) and features such as document preview, job profile management, approval comment highlighting, categorized notifications, upload history tracking, user data export, and integration with external APIs for employee position synchronization. By digitizing the entire process, the application improves administrative efficiency, transparency, and document management across the organization.

The system supports four main user roles:

👑 Super Administrator
👨‍💼 Administrator
📂 Document Controller
👤 Employee

SUPER Workflow

The SUPER workflow follows a structured review and approval process to ensure every job profile proposal is properly validated and authorized before becoming an official job description document.

  • 📝 Job Profile Submission – Employees (Grade 4 and below) create and submit a job profile proposal through the SUPER system using the standardized document template.
  • 🔎 Department Review – The Head of Work Unit (Grade 3) reviews the submitted job profile for completeness, accuracy, and compliance. Proposals requiring revision are returned to the employee.
  • ✅ Stage I Approval – The Department VP (Grade 2) evaluates and approves the reviewed proposal. Rejected proposals are returned for revision.
  • ✔️ Stage II Approval – The Department SVP (Grade 1) performs the final review and approval. If necessary, the proposal is returned to the employee for further revision.
  • 📄 Job Profile Finalization – Once fully approved, the proposal is finalized and officially recorded as the employee's Job Profile document within the SUPER system.

About Project

By integrating these functions into a single platform, DivisiHR helps organizations streamline HR administration, reduce manual workloads, and enhance operational efficiency.

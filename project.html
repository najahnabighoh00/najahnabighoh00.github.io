STIP Sipencatar 🎓

About Project

A web-based admission system that manages the entire cadet recruitment process, including applicant registration, document submission, admission selection, examination schedules, and announcement of admission results.

The system supports two primary user roles:

👤 Prospective Cadet (Applicant)

Prospective cadets can create an account to participate in the admission process. Through the system, they can:

  • ✔ Create and activate an admission account.
  • ✔ Complete personal and academic information.
  • ✔ Upload all required admission documents.
  • ✔ Apply for the active admission period.
  • ✔ Monitor the status of document verification and selection stages.
  • ✔ View admission results and selection announcements.


👨‍💼 Administrator

Administrators manage the entire admission process through a centralized administration panel with the following modules:

  • ✔ Dashboard – Monitor admission activities and overall system status.
  • ✔ General Reference – Manage master data and general reference information.
  • ✔ Academic Reference – Configure academic data and admission settings.
  • ✔ Master Data – Manage required documents, upload limits, selection stages, examination types, and admission periods.
  • ✔ Selection Management – Verify applicant documents, manage selection stages, and determine successful applicants.
  • ✔ Reports – Generate admission transaction reports and administrative summaries.
  • ✔ System Management – Configure system settings and operational parameters.
  • ✔ User Management – Manage administrator accounts, roles, and access permissions.
  • ✔ Landing Page Management – Update public information, announcements, and website content.
  • ✔ Application Settings – Manage system configuration, administrative requirements, applicant password reset, applicant email removal, and manual applicant account activation.

The application simplifies the booking process by allowing users to register, submit reservation requests, select available facilities, and manage their bookings, while providing administrators with tools to efficiently manage assets, reservations, and public service information.


The system supports two primary user roles:

👤 Customer

  • ✔ Browse available public facilities and accommodations.

  • ✔ View available rooms, facilities, and booking costs.
  • ✔ Submit online reservation requests.
  • ✔ Review booking details before confirmation.

  • ✔ Receive booking status updates and reservation information.

Administrators manage the entire reservation system through a centralized administration panel with the following modules:

  • ✔ Dashboard – Monitor reservation activities, facility availability, and overall system performance.
  • ✔ User Management – Manage customer accounts and administrator access.
  • ✔ Asset Management – Manage public facilities, accommodation assets, and asset availability.
  • ✔ Asset Locking – Block or reserve assets that are temporarily unavailable for booking.
  • ✔ Reservation Management – Process reservation requests and monitor booking status.
  • ✔ Reservation Cancellation – Review and manage customer cancellation requests.
  • ✔ Cancellation Settings – Configure reservation cancellation policies and rules.
  • ✔ Transaction List – Monitor booking transactions and payment records.
  • ✔ Feedback Management – Manage customer feedback, suggestions, and service evaluations.
  • ✔ Gallery Management – Manage facility images displayed on the website.
  • ✔ News Management – Publish announcements and news updates.
  • ✔ Public Services – Manage public service information and facility details.
  • ✔ System Settings – Configure application settings and operational parameters.

Job Tender 📋

About Project

Job Tender is a web-based internal recruitment management system developed for Petrokimia Gresik to streamline employee career mobility and internal hiring through a centralized platform. The system enables work units to publish job vacancies while allowing employees to apply for available positions online.


The platform manages the complete recruitment lifecycle, from job posting, application submission, multi-stage candidate verification, competency assessment, interview scheduling, to recruitment announcements. With role-based access control (RBAC), each user is provided with features and permissions according to their responsibilities, ensuring a secure, transparent, and efficient recruitment process.

The system supports three main user roles:

👨‍💼 Administrator, 🏢 Work Unit, 👤 Employee

Job Tender Workflow

The recruitment process follows a structured workflow to ensure every applicant is evaluated fairly and consistently before proceeding to the next stage.

  • 🏢 Job Vacancy Creation – The Administrator or Work Unit publishes available internal positions.
  • 👤 Employee Application – Employees submit applications for open positions.
  • ✅ Initial Screening – Applications are reviewed based on basic eligibility requirements.
  • 📄 Administrative Verification – Supporting documents and administrative requirements are validated.
  • 🎯 Competency Assessment – Candidates are evaluated based on qualifications and competencies.
  • 📅 Interview Scheduling – Qualified candidates are scheduled for interviews by the Administrator or Work Unit.

PTS 👥

About Project

PTS (Pemangku Tugas Sementara) is a web-based workforce assignment management system developed for PT Petrokimia Gresik to streamline the submission, approval, and monitoring of temporary assignment requests (Pemangku Tugas Sementara/PTS). The platform enables employees to submit assignment proposals digitally while allowing authorized approvers to review, validate, and approve requests through a structured workflow.


The system supports the complete assignment lifecycle, including proposal submission, multi-level approval, assignment letter management, notification services, and real-time monitoring. It also accommodates PGS (Pengganti Sementara) and PLT (Pelaksana Tugas) assignment scenarios, with dedicated workflows for proposals involving or not involving employee replacement, as well as short-term assignments of less than 29 days. By centralizing the process, the application improves administrative efficiency, transparency, and document traceability across the organization.

The system supports eight main user roles:

👨‍💼 Administrator
📝 Drafter
🔎 Reviewer
✅ Approver
🏢 BANG SDM
🤝 HRBP
💰 Remuneration
👤 Employee

PTS Workflow

The PTS workflow follows a structured approval and evaluation process to ensure every temporary assignment proposal is reviewed, validated, and authorized by the appropriate stakeholders before the official assignment letter is issued.

  • 📝 Proposal Submission – The Drafter creates and submits a PGS/PTS proposal.
  • 🔎 Proposal Review – The Reviewer validates the submitted proposal for completeness and compliance.
  • ✅ Approval – The Approver reviews and authorizes eligible proposals.
  • 🏢 HR Evaluation – BANG SDM evaluates organizational and workforce requirements.
  • 💰 Final Evaluation – Remuneration and BANG SDM perform the final evaluation before assignment issuance.
  • 📄 Assignment Letter Generation – The system generates the Temporary Assignment Letter (ST).
  • ✔️ ST Approval – The assignment letter is officially approved and validated before becoming effective.
  • 🚀 PETROSTAR Integration – The approved assignment data is automatically synchronized with the PETROSTAR system.

SUMON 📡

About Project

SUMON (Subsidiary Monitoring System) is a web-based monitoring platform developed for PT Petrokimia Gresik to centralize the management and oversight of subsidiary companies, affiliated companies, and the YPG Group. The system provides comprehensive insights into company profiles, executive positions, organizational performance, vacant positions, upcoming retirement periods, and executive tenure monitoring.


Through role-based access control (RBAC), authorized users can manage master data, monitor subsidiary performance reports, receive notifications, and access interactive dashboards that support strategic decision-making and improve corporate governance across the organization.

The system provides role-based access for users across subsidiary companies, affiliated companies, and the YPG Group. Current user roles include:

👑 Super Administrator
👨‍💼 Administrator
🏢 SVP HR
💰 Remuneration Department
💡 Innovation Department
📊 Portfolio Department
👔 Subsidiary Executive
👨‍💻 Subsidiary Staff
🤝 Affiliate
🛡️ K3PG
🎓 YPG
👀 Visitor
📈 SVP Planning & Control
👥 PSDMO Department

SUMON Workflow

The SUMON workflow provides a centralized process for managing subsidiary company data, organizational structures, and performance reports. It ensures that subsidiary information is properly maintained and that business performance reports are consistently collected, monitored, and made available for management evaluation.

  • 🏢 Subsidiary Registration – The Super Administrator registers new subsidiary companies, affiliated companies, or YPG Group entities into the system.
  • 🏛️ Organization Setup – The organizational structure and related information for each subsidiary are configured to establish the company profile within the system.
  • ⚙️ Management Configuration – Management positions and organizational data are maintained to ensure accurate monitoring and reporting.
  • 📊 Performance Report Import – The Super Administrator uploads performance reports through Excel, allowing the system to process and store business performance data for each subsidiary.
  • 📈 Performance Monitoring – The system automatically organizes the imported data into comprehensive business performance reports, including:
    • Real Performance Report (Current Year)
    • RKAP Performance Report
    • Real Performance Report (Previous Year)
    • Production Report
    • Sales Report
    • Profit & Loss Report
    • Financial Position Report
    • Cash Flow Report
    • Financial Ratio Report
